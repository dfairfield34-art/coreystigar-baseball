Common Questions

Everything You Need
to Know

What ages and levels do you train?
Youth through college-level athletes of all skill levels. Whether your child is picking up a bat for the first time or preparing for a college showcase, Coach Corey has a program built for where they are right now.
How long are sessions?
Private lessons run 30 minutes. That's enough time for focused, intentional reps without losing quality. Consistent shorter sessions outperform occasional marathon practices.
Where do sessions take place?
Two Central Valley locations: Riverbank (2301–2355 Novi Dr, 95367) and Modesto (4606 Elm St, 95356). Both are fully equipped indoor facilities — training continues rain or shine.
What should we bring?
Bat, batting gloves, helmet, and cleats or turf shoes. If your athlete doesn't have their own equipment yet, reach out before your session and Coach Corey will set you up.
Do you offer group training?
Yes. In addition to private lessons, group sessions and team training are available. Group work is great for building competitive instincts and learning from peers at a similar level.
How do I book a session?
Book online at professional-baseball-training.square.site, call 408-701-8030, or DM @coachcoreystigar on Instagram. Sessions are Mon–Sat, 9 AM–8 PM.

The Process

How We
Build Players

01

Assess

Every athlete starts with an honest evaluation — mechanics, physical baseline, mental game, and goals. No assumptions, no cookie-cutter programs.
